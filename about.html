Our Mission:

To provide a secure and convenient way for students to cast their ballots in public elections. The system will enable students to cast their votes without having to leave their homes or wait in line at school. It will also provide an audit trail for each vote to ensure its accuracy and integrity.

Our Strengths:

  1. Increased Voter Turnout: The system can increase voter turnout by making it easier for voters to cast their ballots from any location.
  2. Accurate Results: With this system, the results are instantly tallied and can be verified more quickly and accurately than with traditional paper-based methods.
  3. Cost-Effective: The system can be less expensive than traditional paper-based methods requiring fewer resources and personnel.
  4. Secure: The system is highly secure and can use encryption and other security measures to ensure that votes are counted accurately.
